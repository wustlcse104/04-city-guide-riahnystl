Ri A Han, photographer and journalist who has lived in New York City, introduces new york city as a guide.

My life includes a countless number of books, pen, and blank notepads, and I always enjoy my solitude and temporary captivity. I observe, collect, and create. Good work isn't created in a vacuum, and that creativity is always the result of a mind connected to other minds and other various influences" Ri A Han Born in Hamburg, Germany, Ria Han is an aspiring marketer and creative consultant based in New York and South Korea. She started her fashion photography blog Smart & Casual as a high school junior in 2010. She received a BFA Conway/ Proetz Scholarship from Washington University in St. Louis in Missouri in 2013. Her work has been featured in Weitman Gallery at Sam Fox School of Washington University in St. Louis and Student Life (Washington University independent newspaper). She is currently a junior at Washington University in St. Louis, Missouri.

New York is home to the headquarters of the United Nations and an important center for international diplomacy and has been known as the cultural and financial capital of the world. Also, New York has dominant influence upon commerce, finance, media, art, fashion, research, technology, education, and entertainment industries
